The concept

Below Bergen, the tide turns.

Atmosphere

A pirate tavern built around warm candlelight, dark wood, nautical relics, and a sense of entering a secret room beneath the coast.

Drinks

Rum-led cocktails, stormy signatures, local beer, bold alcohol-free serves, and a menu designed for groups that want a night with a little theatre.

Ritual
